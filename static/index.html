Why alignment matters

AI decision aids are increasingly deployed in high-stakes contexts, but humans won't delegate to systems they can't trust. Current language models have fixed, opaque value systems baked in from training data.

ALIGN enables dynamic, personalized alignment of LLM decision-makers to specific human values and an interactive UI for comparing the results. Pick a scenario below, choose a value profile, and see how the decision changes.
